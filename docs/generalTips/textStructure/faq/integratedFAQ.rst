Writing integrated FAQ
======================

Now that you’ve had a little experience with FAQs, writing them for every calculator shouldn’t be too bad. Hopefully, after the initial hump of learning something new, this system should be a little easier to use, as calculator texts can have a bit more of a predefined structure. 

Please check the :ref:`Edit calculator text > FAQ <faqReferencingFAQinMainText>` for the rest of the guide on integrating FAQs.
